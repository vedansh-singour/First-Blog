key takeaways

Business attire is the clothing that is acceptable at work. Business attire can influence the impression of your boss, coworkers, and customers about your capabilities and professionalism. Appropriate business attire can vary from company to company and even from job to job within that company. There are several levels of formality, ranging from casual to business formal.
